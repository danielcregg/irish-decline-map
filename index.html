Irish Language Speakers by County

Percentage of population who can speak Irish (2011-2022)

About this Map

This interactive map shows the percentage of the population who can speak Irish in each county of Ireland from 2011 to 2022, based on Census data from the Central Statistics Office (CSO).

Key Findings:

  • Hover over counties to see exact percentages
  • Use the year selector to see changes over time
  • Darker colors indicate higher percentages of Irish speakers
  • Data shows general decline in most counties from 2011-2022
